# FAQ

**What is Rukh za Lyudyanist?**
It is a charity foundation ("Movement for Humanity") focused on humanitarian work. Its official website is [hmfund.org](https://hmfund.org).

**How can I donate?**
Visit the website's Donate section and choose a donation method, including supported cryptocurrencies with QR codes.

**Can I donate with cryptocurrency?**
Yes — the Donate section provides cryptocurrency addresses and QR codes.

**How do I know my donation is used responsibly?**
The website publishes transparency information, an anti-fraud notice and activity reports in the Reports section.

**Where can I find the foundation's legal documents?**
On the Documents page of the website, along with the privacy policy, terms of use and donation terms.

**How do I contact the foundation?**
Use the Contact section on [hmfund.org](https://hmfund.org).
