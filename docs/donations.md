# How to Donate

The Rukh za Lyudyanist foundation accepts donations through several methods, including cryptocurrency.

## Steps

1. Go to [hmfund.org](https://hmfund.org).
2. Open the **Donate** section.
3. Choose your preferred donation method.
4. For crypto donations, scan the displayed QR code or copy the wallet address.
5. Read the donation terms before completing your contribution.

## Cryptocurrency donations

The Donate section displays cryptocurrency addresses with QR codes for convenient giving. Always confirm you are on the official website ([hmfund.org](https://hmfund.org)) before sending any funds.

## Donation terms and disclaimers

Before donating, please review:

- The **donation terms** page.
- The **anti-fraud notice**.
- The **crypto disclaimer**.

These pages explain how donations are used and how to donate safely.
