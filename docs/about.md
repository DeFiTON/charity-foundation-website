# About the Foundation

**Rukh za Lyudyanist** ("Movement for Humanity") is a charity foundation focused on humanitarian work.

## Mission

The foundation pursues humanitarian goals across several areas of activity, aiming to help people in need and support communities.

## Values

The foundation's work is built on transparency, accountability and care for the people it serves.

## Transparency

The website includes dedicated transparency information and an anti-fraud notice so donors can give with confidence. Activity and impact reporting is published in the Reports section.

## Documents

The foundation's statute and official documents are available on the website's Documents page.

## Learn more

Visit the official website at [hmfund.org](https://hmfund.org) for the full picture of the foundation's mission, reports and legal information.
