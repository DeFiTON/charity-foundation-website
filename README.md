# Charity Foundation Website — Rukh za Lyudyanist

> Official website of the "Rukh za Lyudyanist" (Movement for Humanity) charity foundation — humanitarian mission, transparency, reporting and crypto-friendly donations.

[![Website](https://img.shields.io/badge/Website-hmfund.org-1f6feb)](https://hmfund.org)

## What it is

This is the **official website of the Rukh za Lyudyanist charity foundation**. It presents the foundation's humanitarian mission, areas of activity, transparency commitments, legal documents and donation options — including cryptocurrency donations with QR codes.

The site is designed to give donors clear, trustworthy information and a simple way to support the foundation's work.

## Key sections

- **Mission** — the foundation's humanitarian goals and areas of activity.
- **About** — who the foundation is and the values behind it.
- **Transparency** — trust signals and an anti-fraud notice.
- **Donate** — donation options, including cryptocurrency addresses with QR codes.
- **Reports** — activity and impact reporting.
- **Documents** — statute and official documents.
- **Legal pages** — privacy policy, terms of use, donation terms, anti-fraud notice and crypto disclaimer.
- **Contact** — ways to reach the foundation.

## How to donate

1. Visit [hmfund.org](https://hmfund.org).
2. Open the **Donate** section.
3. Choose a donation method — including supported cryptocurrencies.
4. Scan the QR code or copy the wallet address.
5. Review the donation terms before sending.
